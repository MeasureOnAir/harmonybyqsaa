Artists

Visharada Nirosha Virajini

Nirosha Virajini is an award-winning artist who is a vocalist, a musician, and a music director with a career spanning over 23 years. She is a versatile singer who has sung songs in several languages such as Sinhalese, Tamil, Hindi, and Malayalam. She was awarded the Best Female Vocalist as a playback singer at the Presidential Awards and Sarasaviya Awards in 2000, 2002, and 2016.

Sanka Dineth

Sanka Dineth De Silva is a Sri Lankan pop music singer-songwriter and composer. Sanka won the sixth place at the Sirasa Superstar Season 2 in 2008. As a result of popiularity he gained trough Superstar, he was selected to promotes several promenet brands in Sri Lanka including Lankabell, Elephant Cream Soda and Maliban Biscuits. for His debut album "Sankranthi" has been released in 2009.

Visharada Shirley Waijayantha

Kalabhooshana Visharad Shirley Waijayantha Gunasinghe is a Sri Lankan singer, musician, music instructor and music teacher by profession. He is one of the founding members of Bellwood College of Music in Sri Lanka, Waijayantha has produced several leading singers to the Sri Lankan classical music industry in a career spanning more than four decades.

Nalaka Sajee with Grade 14

Nalaka who has mesmerised many a musical fan in Sri Lanka with his performances in public shows and live musical programmes on television is a music director, performer and graduate teacher. Sajee, a graduate in music from Sri Jayawardenapura University took a keen interest in music and dedicated himself mastering a number of musical instruments mainly string ones. He is a master performer in both classical and modern popular music.
