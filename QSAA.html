Quantity Surveying Alumni Association

Quantity Surveying Alumni Association (QSAA) is a registered chapter of Alumni Association of University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. Quantity Surveying (QS) Graduates who are alumni of Department of Building Economics of the University of Moratuwa are entitled to become members of QSAA, University of Moratuwa.

Quantity Surveying Alumni Association (QSAA) has its registered office at the Department of Building Economics, University of Moratuwa, Katubedda, Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

Our primary aim is to support the University, especially the Department of Building Economics to bring its Quantity Surveying Degree Programme to the forefront in international arena in terms of quality, recognition and achievements. As a part of that, we discuss, share and enlighten new developments/ activities/ experiences and work towards enhancement of welfare and well being of alumni residing and practicing all over the world. In addition we assist the staff and students in various curricular and extracurricular activities.
