Event Mission

The mission of the event is to raising a fund to support the Department of Building Economics of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, to bring its Quantity Surveying degree programme to the forefront in the international arena in terms of quality, recognition, and achievements and welfare and wellbeing of the university students and the alumni residing and practicing all over the world.
